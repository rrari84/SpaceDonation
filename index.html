Space Collection for Housing Helpers

Help Homelessness around the world by collecting space materials! Your score will be converted to a suggested donation amount for Housing Helpers Paypal.

Use arrow keys to move your spaceship and collect all materials. Each material has different point values!

Iron: 10pts | Aluminum: 20pts | Lithium: 30pts | Diamond: 50pts

This game supports Housing Helpers through suggested donations. All donations are processed securely through PayPal.
